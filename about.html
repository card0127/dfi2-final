Lise Presseault is a Canadian born photographer who is best known for her many award winning photo exhibits. From her stunning landscapes to her compelling portraits, Presseault’s work exhibits an honesty and beauty that connect with viewers. Working primarily on location, Presseault travels worldwide shooting for leading companies and agencies.

Lise is based in Ottawa, Ontario and volunteers to most of the local photo clubs in order to expand her knowledge and horizons.
